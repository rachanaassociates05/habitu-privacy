Habitu - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services. This includes:

  • Account Information: Email, name, profile photo from Firebase Authentication.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To show personalized ads (AdMob)

3. Third-Party Services

Habitu uses the following services:

  • Firebase Authentication

  • Google AdMob

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share limited data with trusted service providers (Google Firebase, AdMob).
